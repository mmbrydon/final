New Zealand Police Recruit Their Newest Member, A Tiny Guinea Pig

ANA LUISA SUAREZ


You’ve heard of police dogs, but have you ever heard of police guinea pigs?

Probably not, but they’re just the newest line of defense, if you live in New Zealand that is!

You’re probably wondering how a guinea pig can stop crime and help others. The answer is simple: he is here to motivate!

Elliot was hired by the New Zealand police department as their new mascot to crack down on speeding drivers. How? The police department has been sharing Elliot’s work on social media, even quoting him as saying,

“I am not too fast and that’s the way I like it — slow is safe!”

"Elliot wants drivers to avoid any car-nage..."
-New Zealand Police

I’d have to say I agree with Elliot! It is better to be safe than sorry. I might get laughed at by my friends, but I try my very best to stay well within the speed limit.

Elliot first started his duty after the Easter Bunny got excessive phone calls over the Easter holidays.

When he’s not too busy scolding drivers, Elliot enjoys munching on some grass.

If you thought this police guinea pig was just too cute, you’ll also adore this little bunny that made Zootopia a reality, Miss Bunz the police bunny!
